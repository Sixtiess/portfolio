Liam Hickey's CS Project Portfolio

I have always loved math and science. They provide me with a way to describe and understand the world in a level of detail that words could never do. I like writing code that can help me do something, like calculators or drawing tools. Also, I have spent many hours playing video games, and using code to create my own is a very rewarding experience.

Go

Drive your car along a neverending road. Along the way, make sure to avoid zombies and rocks in your way. If you take damage, repair your car at the next gas station. Only the strongest will survive. Made in collaboration with Milo Rencher and Simon Rencher.
